Allows you to create a swap:

  • You fill in all the required fields.
  • Important: If you are the initiator of the swap, you must click on the checkbox and enter the key. Be sure to memorize it. If you are not the initiator of the swap, then do not click on the checkbox and get the swap key from the initiator.
  • Minimum swap time 3 hours. For the initiator, the swap ends a day later.
  • To withdraw funds when swap is canceled, you must wait for the swap to expire

Swap Process Ideology
  1. 1. Party in Network A (initiator) creates a swap.
  2. 2. The other party in Network B verifies, takes a hash of the key, and creates the swap.
  3. 3. The initiator logs into Network B, confirms the swap, and withdraws assets.
  4. 4. The other party goes to Network A and confirms the swap.
